Game Information

Everything you need to know about Fateshaper - from gameplay mechanics to guides and tutorials.

Game Overview

Fateshape reimagines the classic game of rock, paper, scissors by integrating strategic RPG elements, offering players a unique blend of tactical decision-making and engaging gameplay.

Gameplay

Experience where players strategically manipulate their stats to achieve a maximum score of 100, influencing the game's outcome and their opponents' fates.

Character Stats

Strength

This stats determines how many points a user will gain after each round.

Luck

A stats that determine whether the user scores a point or not based on probability.

Speed

A stats that determine whether the user scores a point or not based on speed.

Frequently Asked Questions

What are the system requirements?

This strategic RPG game requires a minimum of 4 GB RAM, a dual-core processor with a speed of 2.0 GHz, and a graphics card with at least 1 GB VRAM to ensure smooth gameplay as players aim to achieve up to 100 scores through dynamic stat manipulation.

Is there a subscription fee?

Experience an engaging strategic RPG where players compete to achieve up to 100 points by skillfully manipulating stats to outsmart opponents, all without any subscription fees.

Can I play offline?

No.

Is there cross-platform play?

Yes, Fateshaper supports cross-platform play between PC, and Mobile.
